Soniah Supplier Company Ltd - Your Trusted Partner in Tools and Equipment

Established in the heart of Bugesera, Kanzenze, Soniah Supplier Company Ltd has been a cornerstone of quality and reliability in Rwanda's hardware industry. We pride ourselves on offering a wide range of tools and equipment tailored to meet the needs of construction professionals, farmers, and homeowners alike. Our commitment to excellence and customer satisfaction drives us to continuously innovate and expand our offerings, ensuring that every Rwandan has access to the tools they need to succeed.

At Soniah Supplier Company Ltd, we believe in building a brighter future for Rwanda, one tool at a time. Join us on this journey of growth, innovation, and community empowerment.

. Our Mission

To empower Rwandan builders, farmers, and homeowners by providing reliable, durable, and accessible tools, fostering innovation and growth in every community.

. Our Vision

To be Rwanda’s leading hardware store, recognized for quality, trust, and commitment to supporting sustainable development nationwide.
